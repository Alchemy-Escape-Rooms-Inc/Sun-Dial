-This custom button 3D print serves as a shield/housing for SPST push button
in the Sun-Dial design. 

1) The push button goes in the center and is secured using the nut it comes with.

2) Add M2 threaded inserts 6mm in length into the guide pins' centers (which will 
allow you to mount the pins to the 3D printed top). 

3) Fit the guide pins through the custom button 3D printed base and add the springs
over them. (Springs: 7.5mm diameter, 10mm length)

4) Add the top over the guide pins and proceed to secure the assembly using M2 screws.

5) The custom button is now ready to be mounted onto the Sun Dial prop.
